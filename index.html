The Tik-Tokification of Popular Music in the 2020's

How the landscape of popular music has been shifted by TikTok

Matt DiDomenico

12/14/23

The music industry has been turned on its head by the inclusion of social media mareting. However it wasn't until recently until we saw this at the utmost via the explosion of TikTok. With being a music-based platform, when a video goes viral, it's often accompansied by a song. As shown in the chart, this results in the songs themselves getting major boosts in streams and hits. According to veteran artist and producer Chris Seefried, record labels are now encouraging their artists to make music that is more likely to find success on TikTok. "You have artists that are purposefully making songs that have these quick, loopable, earworm qualities to them. Labels have seen songs get found on TikTok and end up getting chart placements. They want that for themselves now."
